Wolves

Wolves can live up to 13 years in the wild and 16 years in captivity.

  • Scientific Name: Canis lupus
  • Average Length: 4.5 to 6.5 feet dependent on sex
  • Average Height: 26 to 32 inches
  • Habitat: North America, Europe, Africa, Asia. Their habitats are shrinking.

Wolves are large canines that tend to run in larger packs. Though they are related to dogs please do not approach as such.

Cats

Cats have an organ that allows them to taste scents. When you think your cat is sneering at you, they're just smelling your air.

  • Scientific Name: Felis catus
  • Average Length: 18 inches
  • Average Height: 9 to 10 inches
  • Habitat: Literally everywhere

Domestic cats are small four legged animals. Cats are very intelligent creatures, and some believe that cats tricked humans into domesticating them for food and shelter.
